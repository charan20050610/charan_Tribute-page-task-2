Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

About

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and the 11th President of India. He played an important role in India's space and missile programs. He inspired millions of students through his knowledge, simplicity, and dedication.

Achievements

  • Known as the Missile Man of India
  • 11th President of India (2002–2007)
  • Contributed to ISRO and DRDO
  • Received Bharat Ratna
